The Premise

Reading a deal memo teaches the what. Building the model teaches the why.

Every deal in this library has a live underwriting model attached. Pull the cap rate higher. Push rent escalations lower. Add a hundred basis points to debt cost. The IRR, equity multiple, and DSCR recalculate in front of you.

Real underwriting is not a static memo — it's a conversation between assumptions and reality. Use these models to feel where the sensitivity actually lives in each deal, and you'll start to recognize the pattern the next time you see it in your own work.
